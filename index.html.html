Length Converter

Basic Information

  • 10 Millimeter (mm) = 1 Centimeter (cm)
  • 10 Centimeters (cm) = 1 Decimeter (dm)
  • 10 Decimeters (dm) = 1 Meter (m)
  • 10 Meters (m) = 1 Decameter (dam)
  • 10 Decameters (dam) = 1 Hectameter (hm)
  • 10 Hectameters (hm) = 1 Kilometer (km)
  • 304.8 Millimeters (mm) = 1 Foot (ft)
  • 25.4 Millimeters (mm) = 1 Inch (in)
  • 1609344 Millimeters (mm) = 1 Mile (mi)
  • 914.4 Millimeters (mm) = 1 Yard (yd)

Weight Converter

Basic Information

  • 10 Milligrams (mg) = 1 Centigram (cg)
  • 10 Centigrams (cg) = 1 Decigram (dg)
  • 10 Decigrams (dmg) = 1 Gram (g or gm)
  • 10 grams (g or gm) = 1 Decagram (dag)
  • 10 Decagrams (dag) = 1 Hectogram (hg)
  • 10 Hectograms (hg) = 1 Kilogram (kg)
  • 100 Kilograms (kg) = 1 Quintal (Q)
  • 10 Quintals = 1 Metric Tonne (T)
  • 1 Gram = 1000 Milligrams
